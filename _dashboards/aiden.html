🏥 Clinical Exposure

Hospital volunteering, shadowing a physician, EMT certification (available in 11th grade). Shows genuine interest in medicine — not just the idea of it.

🔬 Research Experience

Many university labs accept high school students after 10th grade for summer programs. A published abstract or poster is a major differentiator.

🤝 Community Health Impact

Aiden's homeless outreach has massive potential here. Expanding it to focus on health — free screenings, hygiene kits, health education — transforms participation into leadership.

🧬 Academic Competitions

Biology Olympiad, HOSA (Health Occupations Students of America), or a Science Fair project with a health/biology focus reinforces academic commitment.

💡 The Key Insight

UC admissions readers don't just want to see what a student did — they want to see the story it tells. A student with 10 random activities is less compelling than a student with 4–5 connected activities that show genuine passion, deepening commitment, and real impact.
